About me

Elijah, a first-class Electrical and Electronics Engineering graduate, is passionate about advancing renewable energy systems and driving sustainable solutions for underserved communities. With a solid foundation in researching solar mini-grids, energy storage systems, and the intersection of technology, energy, and education, Elijah is dedicated to addressing renewable energy challenges through innovation and collaboration.
In addition to his engineering expertise, Elijah is a skilled full-stack web developer adept at creating dynamic and user-friendly applications. His technical proficiency spans front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, alongside back-end frameworks like PHP, MySQL, Node.js, and Laravel. He pairs these skills with a keen eye for code optimization and solving complex problems.
Elijah’s creativity extends to graphic design, where he excels in tools like Photoshop and Canva, and animation, particularly stop-motion techniques. This unique blend of technical expertise and creative vision positions Elijah as a versatile professional eager to contribute to impactful projects in renewable energy and web development.
